Iris Plant Species

Summary

We're trying to classify the species of the flower based on: sepal length, sepal width, petal length, and petal width.

Features

  1. Sepal length
  2. Sepal width
  3. Petal length

  4. Petal width

  5. Number of samples: 150

  6. Target variable (discrete): 50x Setosa, 50x Versicolor, 50x Virginica

Results

All three classifiers scored above 90%.

The accuracy closely matches that found in the tensorflow example. as well as other references.
